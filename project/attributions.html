Historical & Cultural Information

All historical and cultural information about Kisyombe Village, the Sichali clan, Chiondo Lagoon, and traditional ceremonies is provided by:

  • Elders of Kisyombe Village - Oral histories and traditional knowledge preserved through generations
  • Paramount Traditional Authority Kyungu - Historical records and chieftainship lineage
  • Sichali Clan Elders - Family history of Wayanga Sichali, Shalonda, and Nkhomo
  • Mkwasi Clan Elders - Information on Chiondo sacred beliefs and traditional priesthood
